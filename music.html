Sp33din'/Swallowed My Key - Slater

Two songs in one video. Both of which are great to just cruise around listening to. For me, it really sets the vibes right for a night cruise. Very indie with a solid beat. Slater was my top artist of 2024 on Spotify.

Aqueous Transmission - Incubus

This song is incredibly unique. The way the song seems to flow like water the entire time is beautiful. Incubus was my top artist for 2023 on Spotify.

What Once Was - Her's

Unique indie song, deep lyrics. The song is named "What Once Was" which mirrors the lyrics. "Baby, I've been there before... waiting for what once was." Many can relate to holding onto the fraying tether of something lost.

Turkey - Clock Tower (full album)

I found this on my For You page on 1/28/2025 and I really like the vibe.

Cemetery Gates - Pantera

The first song I ever deemed my "Favorite". Memorable experiences attached to it and a great live performance.

Lazy Eye - Silversun Pickups

Introduced by a friend. A very memorable and emotional song.

Sex Tape - Deftones

Floaty feeling song. Never gets too aggressive and stays chill. Classic Deftones energy.

Locket - Crumb

Crumb makes ethereal, unique songs. Very distinct sound.

Ghostride - Crumb

Another Crumb song, first one I ever heard by them.

Harder, Better, Faster, Stronger - Daft Punk

This one just hits the ears the right way. Classic.
