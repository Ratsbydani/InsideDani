Sp33din'/Swallowed My Key - Slater

Two songs in one video. Both of which are great to just cruise around listening to. For me, it really sets the vibes right for a night cruise. It's something about the energy that these two songs give off, very indie and have a solid beat. Slater was my top artist of 2024 on Spotify and to me it's very clear as to why.

Aqueous Transmission - Incubus

This song is incredibly unique. The way the song seems to flow like water the entire time is beautiful. Incubus was my top artist for 2023 on Spotify.

What Once Was - Her's

Yet another unique indie song, and my favorite as well. This song is actually super deep. The song is named "What Once Was" which perfectly mirrors the lyrics. My favoite lyrics read, "Baby, I've been there before, I was at the point where all I really wanted was someone, And now I'm still hanging on, I was at the end of every tether waiting for what once was." I think a lot of us can actually relate to this snippet. Holding onto the fraying tether of something or someone wishing you could have what once was.

Breaking The Girl - Red Hot Chili Peppers

I honestly really relate to this song. The main meaning of this song is toxic patterns in relationships and exploring themes of love, betrayal, and self-reflection. He acknowledges hurting someone he cared about when Anthony sings "She was the girl I loved the most". The song's melody and dynamic shifts mirror the emotional highs and lows of the relationship, creating a sense of vulnerability and regret. Fuckin love this guy.

Evergreen - Richy Mitch & The Coal Miners

Locked in a Stalemate with a man who bars no holds. Rock and a hard place, he's battering control. What am I waiting for? Feet planted beneath. My compass, my transport. The song touches on being in a negative cycle. This is the song that best describes my dating life sadly but at least I'm aware. It's about wanting to change and do better but you keep holding yourself back and doing the same things. I gave Justina the nickname Evergreen but never used it.

Useless - Omar Apollo

I think a lot of people understand this song pretty well. There's been a good handful of women that render me useless, left in thought about wanting them and being lead on. No matter what they do, they can't make the other person love them back.
